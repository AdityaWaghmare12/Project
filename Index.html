Heat Flow Through a Cylindrical Pipe

Investigate steady-state radial heat conduction through a cylindrical pipe. Compute heat flux using Fourier's law in cylindrical coordinates, determine total heat loss, and find the temperature at any radial point — all through an interactive, guided simulation.
